 1 : In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions
 drain well. 2 : Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven or large pot over medium high heat. Add the garlic and onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until the onions become translucent, about 5 minutes 3 : Add the beef and cook until browned, about 5-10 minutes, making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks. 4 : Stir in the hot dogs, banana ketchup, tomato paste, beef stock, dissolved flour and 1 cup water. 5 : Reduce heat to low
 simmer, stirring occasionally, until sauce has thickened, about 15 minutes. 6 : Season with sugar, salt and pepper, to taste. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes. 7 : Serve immediately.
