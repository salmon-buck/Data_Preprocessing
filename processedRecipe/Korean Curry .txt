 1 : Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the beef, potatoes, carrots and onions and saute until onions are lightly browned, about 4-5 minutes. 2 : Add 3 1/3 cups water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer until meat is tender, about 15 minutes. 3 : Break curry sauce mix into pieces and add to the Dutch oven, stirring until completely melted. 4 : Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. 5 : Serve immediately over rice or noodles.
