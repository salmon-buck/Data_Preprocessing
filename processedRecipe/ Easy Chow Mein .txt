 1 : In a small bowl, whisk together oyster sauce, soy sauce, garlic, ginger and Sriracha
 set aside. 2 : Heat 1 tablespoon canola oil in a large skillet or wok over medium high heat. Add noodles and cook, stirring constantly, until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes
 set aside. 3 : Heat remaining 1 tablespoon canola oil in the skillet. Add mushrooms, and cook, stirring often, until tender, about 3-4 minutes. Stir in bok choy until just wilted, about 1 minute. 4 : Stir in noodles and oyster sauce mixture until well combined, about 2 minutes. Stir in bean sprouts. 5 : Serve immediately.
