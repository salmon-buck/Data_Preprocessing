 1 : Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium high heat. Add the pancetta and cook, stirring occasionally, until brown and crispy, about 8 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate. 2 : Add the garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute. 3 : Add the onion and cook, stirring often, until tender, about 4-5 minutes.  4 : Add the corn, peas and carrots. Cook, stirring constantly, until heated through, about 1-2 minutes.  5 : Stir in the grated ginger.   : Add the rice and soy sauce and gently toss to combine. Cook, stirring constantly, until heated through, about 2 minutes.  7 : Stir in the green onions and pancetta. 8 : Serve immediately, topped with a fried egg and drizzled with Sriracha, if desired.
