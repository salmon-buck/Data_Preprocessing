 Pork And Asparagus With Hoisin Sauce

 1 : Heat sesame oil in a medium skillet over medium high heat. Add pork and asparagus and cook until the pork has browned and the asparagus is tender-crisp, about 4-5 minutes
 season with salt and pepper, to taste. Transfer to a plate. 2 : Add garlic to skillet and saute until fragrant, about 1 minutes. Stir in hoisin and mirin and cook until sauce has reduced slightly, about 3 minutes. Add pork-asparagus mixture and gently toss to combine. Stir in sambal oelek, if desired. 3 : Serve immediately with brown rice, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds, if desired.
