 1 : Heat a large skillet over medium high heat. Add diced bacon and cook until brown and crispy, about 6-8 minutes. Drain excess fat, transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate. Set aside. 2 : Place corn, potato, bacon slices, onion, garlic and thyme into a 6-qt slow cooker. Stir in chicken stock, season with salt and pepper, to taste. 3 : Cover and cook on low heat for 7-8 hours or high heat for 3-4 hours. Remove thyme sprigs and bacon. 4 : Puree with an immersion blender until desired consistency is reached. Stir in heavy cream, season with salt and pepper, to taste. 5 : Serve immediately, sprinkled with bacon and garnished with chives, if desired.
