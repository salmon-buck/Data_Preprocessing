 1 : Place chicken into a 6-qt slow cooker. 2 : Stir in chicken broth, quinoa, enchilada sauce, green chiles, corn, black beans, cilantro, cumin and chili powder
 season with salt and pepper, to taste. Cover and cook on low heat for 4-6 hours, or until liquid is reduced. 3 : Remove chicken from the slow cooker and shred, using two forks. 4 : Stir chicken into the slow cooker
 top with cheeses. Cover and cook on low heat for an additional 10-20 minutes, or until the cheeses have melted. 5 : Serve immediately, garnished with avocado and tomato, if desired.
