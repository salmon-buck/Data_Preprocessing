 1 : Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Lightly oil two 10-ounce ramekins or coat with nonstick spray. 2 : Divide marinara sauce evenly into the ramekins. Top with eggs, milk, gruyere and Parmesan
 season with salt and pepper, to taste. 3 : Place into oven and bake until egg whites are cooked through, about 10 minutes. 4 : Serve immediately, garnished with basil leaves, if desired.
