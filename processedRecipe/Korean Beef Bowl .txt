 1 : In a small bowl, whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, red pepper flakes and ginger. 2 : Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add ground beef and cook until browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks, drain excess fat. 3 : Stir in soy sauce mixture and green onions until well combined, allowing to simmer until heated through, about 2 minutes. 4 : Serve immediately, garnished with green onion and sesame seeds, if desired.
