 1 : In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, green onions, garlic, sesame oil, ginger, pear and Sriracha, if using. Reserve 1/4 cup and set aside. 2 : In a gallon size Ziploc bag or large bowl, combine soy sauce mixture and steak
 marinate for at least 1 hour to overnight, turning the bag occasionally. Drain the steak from the marinade. 3 : In a large saucepan of 2 cups water, cook rice according to package instructions
 set aside. 4 : Preheat grill to medium high heat. Add steak to grill and cook, flipping once and basting with reserved 1/4 cup marinade until cooked through, about 1-2 minutes on each side. 5 : To serve, spoon rice into the center of a lettuce leaf, taco-style
 top with steak, garnished with sesame seeds, if desired.
