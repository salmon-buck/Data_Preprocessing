 1 : In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions. Stir in peas during the last 1 minute of cooking time, drain well and rinse with cold water. 2 : Place eggs in a large saucepan and cover with cold water by 1 inch. Bring to a boil and cook for 1 minute. Cover eggs with a tight-fitting lid and remove from heat. set aside for 8-10 minutes. Drain well and let cool before peeling and dicing. 3 : In a large bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, parsley, vinegar and Dijon. Stir in pasta, peas, eggs, bell pepper, onion, celery and cheese, season with salt and pepper, to taste. 4 : Cover, place into the refrigerator for 2 hours or up to 2 days. 5 : Serve cold.
