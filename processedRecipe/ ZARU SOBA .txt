 1 : In a large pot of boiling water, cook noodles according to package instructions. Rinse under cold water and drain
 set aside. 2 : While the noodles are cooking, grate one radish in each dipping bowl. Squeeze and drain excess moisture. 3 : In the same dipping bowl, add a handful of green onions and wasabi, to taste. 4 : Add the Soba noodle soup base and stir to combine. 5 : Serve dipping sauce with noodles. 6 : Printer-Friendly Version
